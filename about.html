About Us

Founded in Jan 2021, Ziwa Vita (ဇီဝဗီတ) inspires to bridge the gap between Myanmar community and healthier lifestyle.
Promote health literacy in Myanmar with reliable, evidence-based and up-to-date health-related contents.

We are dedicated to challenge the digital health market in Myanmar with our aspiration and promising well-designed contents to become one of the leading digital health platforms for our valuable audience and partners.
